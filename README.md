## Scoring

**Lowest score wins.** Draft 6 golfers under the $50,000 salary cap. Your team's score is the combined score-to-par of all 6 golfers, adjusted for bonuses and penalties.

```
Final Score = Raw Score + Bonus Shots
  Raw Score   = sum of all 6 golfers' scores to par
  Bonus Shots = best-round + solo-leader + winner bonuses (all negative)
```

### Bonuses

| Bonus | Adjustment | Condition |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Best round of the day | −1 shot | Your golfer posts the **unique** lowest single-round score across the entire field. Ties = no bonus. |
| Solo leader after a round | −1 shot per round | One of your golfers is the **outright** tournament leader after R1, R2, or R3. Ties don't count. |
| Tournament winner | −5 shots | One of your golfers **wins outright** (1st place, made cut, completed all 4 rounds). |

### Penalties

| Penalty | Adjustment | Condition |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Missed cut / DQ | +8 shots | Golfer's 2-round score to par plus an 8-shot penalty. |
| Late withdrawal (never played) | +8 shots | Flat +8 penalty if a golfer withdraws before playing. |

### Draft Rules

- Pick exactly **6 golfers**
- Total salary must be **≤ $50,000**
- Teams are **locked when the tournament begins** — no changes after lock
- One team per account

### Tiebreaker

Teams tied on final score are ranked by fewest bonus shots earned (lower raw score wins the tiebreak). If still tied, teams share the rank.
